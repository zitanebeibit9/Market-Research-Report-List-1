<p><p>The ceramic sputtering targets market is primarily divided into two types: planar targets and rotary targets. Planar targets are flat and typically used for applications requiring uniform coating over a surface. They are suitable for smaller-scale projects and applications with lower deposition rates. On the other hand, rotary targets are cylindrical and designed for higher deposition rates, making them ideal for larger-scale industrial applications. Both types are crucial in various fields, including electronics, optics, and solar energy technologies.</p></p>
