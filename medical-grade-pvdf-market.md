<p><p>The medical-grade PVDF (Polyvinylidene fluoride) market is primarily segmented into two types: PVDF granule and PVDF powder. PVDF granules are solid pellets used in various medical applications, offering excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, ideal for manufacturing components like syringes and valves. PVDF powder, on the other hand, is fine particulate material suitable for processes such as 3D printing and coating, allowing for customized shapes and enhanced surface properties in medical devices. Both forms are critical for ensuring high-performance medical solutions.</p></p>
