<p><p>The immunohistochemistry reagent market encompasses various types essential for tissue analysis. Histological stains are used for visualizing cellular structures and identifying tissues. Blocking sera and reagents prevent non-specific binding, enhancing the accuracy of antibody interactions. Chromogenic substrates convert enzyme reactions into visible signals, facilitating the detection of specific proteins. Other reagents may include secondary antibodies, fixatives, and mounting media, which collectively support the immunohistochemical process and improve diagnostic outcomes in research and clinical settings.</p></p>
