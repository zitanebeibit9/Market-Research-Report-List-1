<p><strong>The qRT-PCR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>qPCR</li><li>dPCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The qRT-PCR market can be divided into two primary types: quantitative PCR (qPCR) and digital PCR (dPCR). qPCR is widely used for quantifying DNA and RNA, offering real-time monitoring of amplification and high-throughput capabilities. In contrast, dPCR provides absolute quantification by partitioning samples into multiple reactions, enhancing sensitivity and accuracy, particularly for rare targets. Both technologies serve critical roles in research, diagnostics, and biotech, catering to varying needs in gene expression analysis and genetic detection.</p></p>
