<p><strong>The Artificial Casings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Collagen Casing</li><li>Cellulose Casing</li><li>Fibrous Casing</li><li>Plastic Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial casings market encompasses several types: </p><p>1. **Collagen Casing**: Made from collagen derived from animal hides, these casings are edible and provide a consistent texture and strength, ideal for various sausages. </p><p>2. **Cellulose Casing**: Often used for smoked sausages, these are non-edible and primarily provide structural integrity during cooking and processing. </p><p>3. **Fibrous Casing**: Formed from plant fibers, these casings are strong and used for larger sausages, designed for peeling after cooking. </p><p>4. **Plastic Casing**: Non-edible and versatile, plastic casings are used for products requiring longer shelf life, suited for various processing methods.</p></p>
