<p><p>The Automotive Premium Interior Leather market primarily comprises two types: Genuine Leather and Synthetic Leather. In contrast, Synthetic Leather, made from materials like polyurethane or polyvinyl chloride, offers a cost-effective, environmentally friendly alternative, mimicking the look and feel of real leather while being more resistant to wear and easier to maintain, appealing to budget-conscious consumers and eco-friendly brands.</p></p>
