<p><strong>The Matte Lipstick Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cream Type</li><li>Fluid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The matte lipstick market offers two primary types: cream and fluid. **Cream-type matte lipsticks** have a rich, buttery texture that glides on the lips smoothly, providing a comfortable wear while maintaining a matte finish. They often offer higher pigmentation and are less drying than other formulas. **Fluid-type matte lipsticks**, on the other hand, feature a lightweight, liquid consistency that quickly dries to a matte finish, offering long-lasting wear but can be more drying. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences for matte lip color.</p></p>
