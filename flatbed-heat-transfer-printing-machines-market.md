<p><p>Flatbed heat transfer printing machines are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operators to position materials and control the printing process, offering lower production rates but greater precision. Semi-automatic machines streamline some processes, allowing for increased efficiency while still requiring human intervention. Fully automatic machines manage all aspects of printing, from material handling to finishing, offering high speed and consistency, making them ideal for large-scale production and complex designs.</p></p>
