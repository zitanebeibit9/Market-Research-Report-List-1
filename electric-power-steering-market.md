<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li><li>REPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) market consists of three primary types: Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS), which integrates the electric motor within the steering column for compact designs; Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS), which places the motor at the pinion gear for improved responsiveness; and Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS), offering the highest level of assistance by mounting the motor on the steering rack. Each type aims to enhance vehicle handling, fuel efficiency, and driver comfort through advanced technology.</p></p>
