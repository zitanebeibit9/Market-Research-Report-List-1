<p><strong>The Molecular Sieve Raw Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A Type</li><li>X Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molecular sieve raw powders are categorized mainly into A Type and X Type, each with distinct properties and applications. A Type molecular sieves have a pore size of about 3 angstroms, making them ideal for selectively adsorbing small molecules like water and certain gases. X Type molecular sieves, on the other hand, possess larger pores around 10 angstroms, allowing for the adsorption of larger molecules, such as hydrocarbons. Both types are crucial in areas like gas processing, petrochemicals, and environmental applications.</p></p>
