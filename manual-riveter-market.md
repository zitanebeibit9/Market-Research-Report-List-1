<p><p>The manual riveter market is segmented into three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual riveters. Automatic riveters operate without user intervention, enhancing production efficiency and precision for high-volume applications. Semi-automatic riveters require some manual input, offering a balance between automation and control, making them suitable for medium-scale operations. Manual riveters are entirely operated by hand, providing versatility for low-volume tasks and repairs. Each type caters to different operational needs and production scales in various industries.</p></p>
