<p><strong>The Electronically Scanned Arrays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronically Scanned Arrays (ESAs) are categorized into active and passive types based on their functioning. Active ESAs integrate transmit/receive modules at each antenna element, enabling precise beam steering and dynamic resource allocation, ideal for advanced radar and communication systems. In contrast, passive ESAs consist of fixed antennas without built-in amplification, relying on external systems for signal processing. This makes them simpler and more cost-effective but limits adaptability and performance compared to their active counterparts in high-demand applications.</p></p>
