<p><strong>The Low Iron Clear Flat Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low iron clear flat glass market primarily encompasses two types: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, resulting in a textured surface, often used for applications like safety glazing and decorative design. In contrast, float glass is made by floating molten glass on tin, yielding a smooth, uniform surface ideal for windows and solar panels. Both types are valued for their clarity, low iron content, and versatility in architectural and industrial applications.</p></p>
