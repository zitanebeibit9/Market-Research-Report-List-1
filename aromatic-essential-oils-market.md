<p><p>The aromatic essential oils market is primarily divided into two types: natural essential oils and synthetic oils. In contrast, synthetic oils are chemically created to mimic natural fragrances and may lack the same therapeutic benefits. Each type serves different consumer preferences, with natural oils often favored for holistic uses while synthetic oils provide cost-effective alternatives in various applications, including cosmetics and scents.</p></p>
