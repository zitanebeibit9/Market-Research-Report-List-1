<p><p>The artificial musk market consists of three main types: Nitro Musk, Polycyclic Musk, and Macrocyclic Musk. Nitro musks are synthetic compounds known for their strong, sweet scents and are often used in perfumes and personal care products. Polycyclic musks are characterized by their complex molecular structures, providing lasting fragrances but raising environmental concerns. Macrocyclic musks, on the other hand, have a more natural scent profile and better biodegradability, making them increasingly popular in eco-friendly formulations. Each type serves distinct purposes within the fragrance industry.</p></p>
