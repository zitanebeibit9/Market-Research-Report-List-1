<p><p>The tire recycling machines market includes various types, primarily "Tire Shredders" and "Tire Pyrolysis Equipment." Tire shredders are used to mechanically break down whole tires into smaller pieces, facilitating easier handling and subsequent processing, such as generating crumb rubber. Tire pyrolysis equipment, on the other hand, involves thermal decomposition of tires in an oxygen-free environment, converting them into valuable products like oils, carbon black, and gases, which can be repurposed for energy or industrial applications.</p></p>
