<p><p>The Counter Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (C-UAV) Defense System market is categorized into two primary types: Detection Systems and Detection and Disruption Systems. Detection Systems focus on identifying and tracking UAVs using radar, infrared, or electro-optical sensors. They are crucial for situational awareness and threat assessment. In contrast, Detection and Disruption Systems not only detect UAVs but also actively neutralize them through methods like jamming, spoofing, or physical interception, ensuring a comprehensive approach to airspace security against unauthorized drones.</p></p>
