<p><ul><li>Straight Bevel Gearbox</li><li>Spiral Bevel Gearbox</li><li>Zerol Bevel Gearbox</li><li>Hypoid Bevel Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bevel gearbox market includes various types based on gear design. Straight bevel gearboxes feature straight teeth, providing reliable performance for moderate speeds. Spiral bevel gearboxes, with their angled teeth, offer smoother operation and higher load capacity. Zerol bevel gearboxes utilize zero-degree angle teeth for quieter efficiency and compact design. Hypoid bevel gearboxes, combining spiral gear features with offset shafts, facilitate high torque and smooth engagement. Each type caters to specific industrial applications, enhancing mechanical power transmission.</p></p>
