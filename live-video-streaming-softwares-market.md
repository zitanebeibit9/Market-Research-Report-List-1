<p><p>The live video streaming software market is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a user's hardware, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant infrastructure and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based solutions operate over the internet, allowing users to access streaming services remotely, easily scale resources, and benefit from lower upfront costs. Both types cater to varying needs, with on-premise favored by enterprises needing security, while cloud-based suits users seeking flexibility and convenience.</p></p>
