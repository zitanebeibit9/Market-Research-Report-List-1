<p><strong>The Electrically Adjustable Focus Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrowetting-Based Liquid Lense</li><li>Liquid Crystal Lense</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrically adjustable focus lenses market includes two primary types: electrowetting-based liquid lenses and liquid crystal lenses. Electrowetting-based lenses utilize a change in surface tension to alter the curvature of a liquid interface, allowing for rapid focus adjustments. In contrast, liquid crystal lenses manipulate the alignment of liquid crystal molecules in response to an electric field, enabling variable optical properties. Both technologies offer advantages in applications such as cameras, projectors, and augmented reality, enhancing image quality and flexibility.</p></p>
