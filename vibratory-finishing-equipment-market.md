<p><strong>The Vibratory Finishing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Linear</li><li>CNC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vibratory finishing equipment is categorized into three main types: centrifugal, linear, and CNC. </p><p>Centrifugal machines use high-speed rotation to enhance polishing and deburring efficiency, ideal for small, complex parts. Linear vibrators utilize a straight-line motion, providing effective mass finishing for larger or bulk components, ensuring consistent results. Meanwhile, CNC systems integrate computerized controls for precise operations, making them suitable for complex geometries and high-volume production, offering flexibility and repeatability in finishing processes. Each type serves distinct applications in manufacturing and metalworking.</p></p>
