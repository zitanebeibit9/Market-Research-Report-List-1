<p><p>The nitrile gloves market consists primarily of two types: powder-free and powdered nitrile gloves. Powder-free nitrile gloves are designed for individuals with sensitivities, providing a barrier without the risk of powder-related irritation. They are commonly used in medical, food handling, and laboratory settings. On the other hand, powdered nitrile gloves feature a cornstarch powder that facilitates easier donning and doffing, making them suitable for industrial applications. Both types offer excellent chemical resistance and durability, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
