<p><p>Wafer Butterfly Valves are essential for regulating fluid flow in various applications and are categorized by their connection types. Flanged ends feature raised or flat flanges for easy bolting to pipelines, ensuring a secure fit. Threaded ends use male or female threads for straightforward installation. Socket ends involve a socket design that allows for a simple push-fit connection, while butt-welded ends require welding the valve directly to the pipe for a robust, permanent connection. Each type serves specific installation and operational needs.</p></p>
