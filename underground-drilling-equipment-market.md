<p><p>The underground drilling equipment market is categorized into three main types: Single Boom Drilling Rig, Double Boom Drilling Rig, and Multi Boom Drilling Rig. Single Boom Rigs are designed for drilling in confined spaces, ideal for narrow tunnels. Double Boom Rigs enhance productivity and efficiency, allowing simultaneous drilling of two holes. Multi Boom Rigs, with multiple booms, maximize output and versatility, suitable for larger or more complex mining operations. Each type caters to specific operational needs and project scales in underground environments.</p></p>
