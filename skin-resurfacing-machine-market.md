<p><p>The skin resurfacing machine market is categorized into two main types: ablative and non-ablative. Ablative devices remove layers of skin to treat deep imperfections, promoting collagen production for significant improvements in texture and tone. Non-ablative machines, on the other hand, work by stimulating underlying skin structures without damaging the surface, resulting in minimal downtime and milder effects. Both types are popular for addressing issues like wrinkles, scars, and pigmentation, catering to varying patient needs and recovery preferences.</p></p>
