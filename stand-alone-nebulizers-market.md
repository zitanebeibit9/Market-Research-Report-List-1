<p><p>The Stand Alone Nebulizers market comprises three main types: Pneumatic Nebulizers, Ultrasonic Nebulizers, and Mesh Nebulizers. Pneumatic Nebulizers use compressed air to convert medication into mist, making them widely used for respiratory treatments. Ultrasonic Nebulizers utilize high-frequency sound waves to aerosolize liquid medications, offering a quieter and faster option. Mesh Nebulizers leverage a mesh surface to create a fine mist and are known for their portability and efficiency, making them ideal for various patient needs in home and clinical settings.</p></p>
