<p><strong>The Foundry Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Casting Machine</li><li>Centrifugal Casting Machine</li><li>Induction Furnace</li><li>Moulding Machine</li><li>Coremaking Machine</li><li>Shot Blasting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foundry equipment market encompasses various machines essential for metal casting processes. Die casting machines create precise metal parts through high-pressure injection. Centrifugal casting machines utilize rotational forces to produce cylindrical components with uniform density. Induction furnaces melt metals efficiently using electromagnetic induction. Moulding machines shape sand or other materials into molds, while coremaking machines produce cores for hollow castings. Shot blasting machines clean and finish castings by propelling abrasive materials, ensuring surface quality and integrity in the final products.</p></p>
