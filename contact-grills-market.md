<p><p>The contact grills market encompasses two main types: Liquid Propane (LP) grills and Natural Gas (NG) grills. LP grills are portable and ideal for outdoor use, offering convenience with refillable propane tanks, making them popular for camping and tailgating. In contrast, NG grills connect directly to home gas lines, providing a constant fuel source and eliminating the need for gas tank replacements. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with LP grills focusing on mobility and NG grills emphasizing continuous use and efficiency.</p></p>
