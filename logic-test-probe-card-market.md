<p><p>The Logic Test Probe Card market encompasses several types designed for specific semiconductor testing needs. Vertical Needle/Tip probe cards feature precise vertical needles that make contact with the chip's pads, ensuring accurate testing. Thin Film MLO (Multi-Layer Organic) probe cards utilize a multi-layer design for better signal integrity and performance at high frequencies. Membrane probe cards rely on flexible membranes to accommodate various chip designs while maintaining reliable contact, making them suitable for diverse applications. Each type addresses unique testing requirements in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
