<p><strong>The Image and Video Capture Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCI/PCIe Capture Cards</li><li>USB Capture Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The image and video capture card market comprises mainly two types: PCI/PCIe capture cards and USB capture cards. PCI/PCIe capture cards offer high-performance video input with low latency, making them ideal for professional setups in gaming, streaming, and video production. They are installed directly on the motherboard for faster data transfer. USB capture cards, on the other hand, provide portability and ease of use, connecting via USB ports, suitable for casual recording and streaming without the need for installation inside a computer.</p></p>
