<p><p>The custom assays market includes various types tailored for specific research needs. Activity assays measure the functional capabilities of a target, while competitive assays determine the concentration of substances by competition with a labeled counterpart. ELISA assays use enzyme-linked antibodies for detection and quantification of proteins. Screening assays facilitate large-scale testing to identify active compounds or genetic anomalies. Additionally, "Others" encompass specialized techniques like multiplex assays, each catering to distinct analytical requirements in biomedical research and diagnostics.</p></p>
