<p><ul><li>In-vivo</li><li>In-vitro</li><li>In-Silico</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Early toxicity testing encompasses three primary market types: in-vivo, in-vitro, and in-silico. In-vivo testing involves assessing the toxicity of substances through live organisms, providing insights into biological responses. In-vitro testing uses cellular or tissue samples to evaluate toxic effects in a controlled laboratory setting, offering rapid and cost-effective results. In-silico testing employs computational models and simulations to predict toxicity based on existing data, streamlining the drug development process while reducing reliance on animal testing. Together, these approaches enhance safety assessments in pharmaceuticals and chemicals.</p></p>
