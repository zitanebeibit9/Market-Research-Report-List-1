<p><p>Crosslinked Polyethylene (PE-X) pipe is categorized into three main types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa is produced through a peroxide method, offering enhanced flexibility and resistance to high temperatures. PE-Xb uses a silane method for crosslinking, providing good strength and chemical resistance. PE-Xc is created via irradiation, known for its cost-effectiveness and stability under pressure. Each type varies in production technique, properties, and applications, catering to diverse plumbing, heating, and industrial needs.</p></p>
