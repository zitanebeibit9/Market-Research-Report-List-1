<p><strong>The Automotive Hypervisor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare Metal Hypervisors</li><li>Hosted Hypervisors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hypervisor market features two primary types: bare-metal hypervisors and hosted hypervisors. Bare-metal hypervisors run directly on the hardware, ensuring optimal performance and resource allocation for automotive applications, making them ideal for critical systems. In contrast, hosted hypervisors operate atop a conventional operating system, providing flexibility and easier management of virtual machines. This type is beneficial for infotainment and non-critical applications, allowing developers to utilize diverse operating environments while maintaining system efficiency and safety.</p></p>
