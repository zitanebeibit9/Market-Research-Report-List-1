<p><p>The advanced and targeted drug delivery market encompasses several innovative systems designed for improved therapeutic efficacy. Liposomes are vesicles that encapsulate drugs, enhancing bioavailability and reducing side effects. Microspheres are small spherical particles that enable sustained release and targeted action. Nanoparticles offer unique properties for precise targeting at the cellular level, enhancing drug penetration and reducing systemic toxicity. Emulsions, combining oil and water phases, facilitate the delivery of hydrophobic drugs, improving stability and absorption. Together, these technologies revolutionize treatment paradigms.</p></p>
