<p><p>The Rare Gas Purifier market is segmented into three main types based on the materials used in purification. Titanium Tube purifiers utilize titanium’s high affinity for impurities, effectively removing contaminants from rare gases. Copper Oxide Tube purifiers leverage copper oxide’s ability to absorb unwanted gases, making them suitable for processes requiring oxygen removal. Molecular Sieve Tube purifiers employ a porous structure to selectively trap and separate molecules based on size, ensuring high purity levels in rare gas applications.</p></p>
