Cable Probe Thermometers rely on a sensor connected by a cable, allowing precise temperature measurement directly in the water. Wireless Floating Thermometers use a buoyant design with built-in sensors, transmitting data wirelessly to a display unit, enhancing convenience and mobility. Both types provide essential temperature readings for pool maintenance, ensuring optimal water conditions for user safety and comfort.</p></p>
