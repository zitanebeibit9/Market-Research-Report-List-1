<p><p>The Double Block and Bleed (DBB) valve market is categorized into Single DBB Valves and Double DBB Valves. Single DBB Valves consist of one valve that offers two sealing points and a bleed valve for safe release of trapped fluids. Conversely, Double DBB Valves feature two separate block valves and a bleed valve, providing enhanced safety and isolation capabilities. These configurations are critical in preventing leaks and managing pressure in pipeline systems across various industries, enhancing operational reliability.</p></p>
