<p><strong>The Paint Stripper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkaline</li><li>Acidic</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint stripper market comprises primarily three types: alkaline, acidic, and others. Alkaline strippers utilize a high pH to break down paint through saponification, making them effective for removing oil-based finishes. Acidic strippers, on the other hand, employ low pH solutions to dissolve paints, particularly useful for metallic surfaces. The "others" category includes biodegradable and solvent-based options that cater to specific applications and environmental concerns. Each type serves diverse needs depending on the paint type and surface material.</p></p>
