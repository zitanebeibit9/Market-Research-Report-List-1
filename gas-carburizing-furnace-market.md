<p><strong>The Gas Carburizing Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Hearth Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnace</li><li>Push Plate Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas carburizing furnace market consists of various types, primarily including the roller hearth continuous gas carburizing furnace and the push plate continuous gas carburizing furnace. The roller hearth type utilizes a series of rollers to transport parts through a controlled atmosphere, offering uniform heating and efficient operation. In contrast, the push plate continuous furnace employs a plate-driven system to move items, ensuring consistent carburizing while accommodating larger components. Both types are vital for enhancing surface hardness in steel components, improving durability and performance.</p></p>
