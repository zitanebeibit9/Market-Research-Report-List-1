<p><p>The rotary bearings market includes various types designed to facilitate smooth rotational movement. Ball bearings use spherical balls to reduce friction and accommodate radial and axial loads. Rolling bearings encompass ball bearings and other forms that allow rolling motion, enhancing efficiency. Roller bearings utilize cylindrical rollers, distributing loads over a larger surface area, offering high load capacity and stability. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, promoting machinery performance, reliability, and longevity while minimizing wear and energy consumption.</p></p>
