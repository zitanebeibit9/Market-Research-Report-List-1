<p><p>The DMS (Driver Monitoring System) Camera for vehicles is categorized into two market types: Active and Passive. Active DMS utilizes real-time monitoring features, such as alerts or intervention systems, to keep drivers engaged and alert, often incorporating cabin sensors and cameras. In contrast, Passive DMS focuses on data collection without direct intervention, analyzing driver behavior and fatigue levels, primarily for post-trip analysis or insurance purposes. Both types aim to enhance safety and reduce accidents related to driver impairment.</p></p>
