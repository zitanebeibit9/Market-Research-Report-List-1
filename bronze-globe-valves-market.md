<p><p>The bronze globe valves market can be categorized into two primary connection types: flanged and threaded connections. Flanged connection valves typically feature flat surfaces with bolt holes, providing robust and secure fittings for high-pressure applications. In contrast, threaded connection valves utilize screw threads for attachment, making them ideal for smaller pipes and applications requiring easy disassembly. Each type caters to specific industry needs, ensuring reliable flow control in plumbing, HVAC, and industrial systems while maintaining durability and resistance to corrosion.</p></p>
