<p><p>A three-phase transformer is a critical device in electrical power systems, designed to efficiently step up or step down voltage levels in three-phase power transmission. Three-phase transformers are widely used in industrial applications, substations, and renewable energy systems, contributing significantly to power distribution efficiency.</p><p>The three-phase transformer market is witnessing robust growth, driven by increasing energy demand and the need for grid modernization.
