<p><p>The Disposable Coin Li-ion Battery market is categorized into four types: LR (Lithium Rechargeable), SR (Silver Oxide), CR (Lithium Coin Cell), and ZA (Alkaline). LR batteries are rechargeable but designed for high-drain devices. SR batteries offer stable voltage and are commonly used in watches. CR batteries have a longer shelf life and are ideal for low-drain applications. ZA batteries, while less common, are often used in clocks and toys, providing a cost-effective solution for low-power needs.</p></p>
