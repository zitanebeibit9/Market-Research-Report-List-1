<p><ul><li>Solid Type Aluminum Electrolyte Capacitor</li><li>Non-Solid Type Aluminum Electrolyte Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum electrolytic capacitor market is divided into two main types: solid type and non-solid type. Solid type aluminum electrolytic capacitors utilize a solid electrolyte, offering enhanced stability, lower equivalent series resistance (ESR), and improved longevity, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Non-solid type capacitors, on the other hand, use a liquid electrolyte, generally providing higher capacitance values and better performance under high voltage but may have shorter lifespans and higher ESR. Each type serves different applications based on performance needs.</p></p>
