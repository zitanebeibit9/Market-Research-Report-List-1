<p><strong>The Smart Sortation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Sortation Systems</li><li>Loop Sortation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Sortation System market is categorized into Linear Sortation Systems and Loop Sortation Systems. Linear Sortation Systems operate in straight lines, facilitating efficient sorting for high-volume operations with minimal space requirements. They are ideal for environments with straightforward sorting needs, such as e-commerce. In contrast, Loop Sortation Systems utilize a circular path, allowing items to be sorted and returned efficiently. This system is beneficial for high-throughput applications, enhancing workflow by maximizing sorting capabilities in confined spaces.</p></p>
