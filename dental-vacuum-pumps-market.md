<p><p>The dental vacuum pumps market consists of two main types: wet vacuum and dry vacuum systems. Wet vacuum pumps utilize water as a sealing fluid, providing efficient suction but requiring more maintenance due to water usage and potential contamination. In contrast, dry vacuum pumps operate without water, offering a cleaner and more environmentally friendly solution, with lower maintenance needs. Both types serve critical functions in dental practices, facilitating effective suction during procedures and enhancing overall patient care.</p></p>
