<p><ul><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desktop security scanner market features three primary types: Pulse Induction (PI), Very Low Frequency (VLF), and Beat-Frequency Oscillation (BFO). Pulse Induction technology is effective in detecting metals in various environments by sending strong magnetic pulses, suitable for deep detection. Very Low Frequency scanners utilize low frequency signals to achieve better sensitivity and discrimination, often favored for security applications. Beat-Frequency Oscillation combines frequencies to improve detection accuracy, making it essential in identifying diverse metallic objects in security settings.</p></p>
