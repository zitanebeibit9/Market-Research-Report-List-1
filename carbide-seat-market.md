<p><p>The carbide seat market primarily includes two main types: cone and round. Cone carbide seats feature a tapered design, allowing for efficient sealing and pressure regulation in various industrial applications. They are often used in valves and fittings. Round carbide seats, on the other hand, provide a flat surface beneficial for uniform contact and stability, commonly employed in compression applications. Both types are essential in ensuring durability and performance in systems requiring high wear resistance and precise sealing capabilities.</p></p>
