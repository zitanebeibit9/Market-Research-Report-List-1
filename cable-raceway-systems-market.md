<p><p>Cable raceway systems are essential for organizing and protecting electrical and communication cables. The market can be divided into two main types: metal and non-metallic. Metal raceways, typically made from materials like aluminum or steel, offer superior strength, durability, and grounding capabilities, making them suitable for industrial applications. Non-metallic raceways, often constructed from PVC or fiberglass, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and easier to install, making them ideal for commercial and residential use where flexibility and aesthetics are prioritized.</p></p>
