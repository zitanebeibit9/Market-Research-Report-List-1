<p><p>The Ceramic Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) Thermistors market primarily comprises two types: Self-Heating Mode and Sensor Mode. In Self-Heating Mode, thermistors generate heat when current flows through them, making them useful for overcurrent protection and controlling heating elements. In contrast, Sensor Mode thermistors respond to temperature changes, providing accurate temperature measurements for applications in HVAC systems, automotive, and consumer electronics. Each mode serves distinct roles in temperature regulation and monitoring, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
