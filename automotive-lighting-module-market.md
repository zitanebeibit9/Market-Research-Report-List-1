<p><strong>The Automotive Lighting Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halogen Lighting</li><li>HID Lighting</li><li>LED Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive lighting module market encompasses three main types: Halogen lighting, HID lighting, and LED lighting. Halogen lighting is the traditional option, offering affordability and ease of replacement but lower efficiency. HID lighting delivers brighter illumination and improved visibility through high-intensity discharge, making it popular for premium vehicles but at a higher cost. LED lighting is becoming increasingly favored due to its energy efficiency, long lifespan, and compact design, paving the way for advanced lighting technologies in modern vehicles.</p></p>
