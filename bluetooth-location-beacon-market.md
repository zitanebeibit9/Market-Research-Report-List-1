<p><p>The Bluetooth location beacon market features three primary types: iBeacon, Eddystone, and AltBeacon. iBeacon, developed by Apple, enables proximity marketing by transmitting signals to nearby devices. Eddystone, supported by Google, offers more flexibility with various data formats, including URLs and telemetry data. AltBeacon, an open-source alternative, allows for customization and adaptability in applications. Each type serves to enhance location-based services, enabling businesses to engage customers through targeted notifications and interactions based on their physical proximity to beacons.</p></p>
