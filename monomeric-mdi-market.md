<p><p>The monomeric MDI (Methylene Diphenyl Diisocyanate) market is divided into solid and liquid types, catering to different applications. Solid MDI is primarily used in rigid foam production, including insulation materials and automotive parts, due to its high structural integrity. In contrast, liquid MDI is favored in flexible foam applications, such as mattresses and cushioning, offering greater adaptability. Both forms are essential in various industries, with solid MDI generally commanding higher prices due to its specialized uses and production complexity.</p></p>
